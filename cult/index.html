Cult@H2



Cultural Activities have been an important part of life at hostel 2. Hostel 2 boasts of having some of the best dancers, musicians, actors, writers, quizzers, speakers, and artists in the institute. Hostel 2 has been in top two positions in Performing Arts Festival a.k.a. PAF for the last few years. The winner of the first Hostel cup and having been positioned fourth in overall general championship last year, H2 continues its path to glory this year too.

Dance@H2

Hostel 2 is home to some of the best dancers of the institute. The dance culture in hostel has been one of the best and continues to get better. The dancers of H2 won 3rd position in Gyrations consecutively for the 2nd year and have bought glory by winning dance competitions in other colleges too.

Dram@H2

Nautanki has always been a special talent of hostel inmates. The wild ones of H2 have always been actively participating in dramatics. Hostel 2 boasts of some of the notable alumni actors of the institute. Be it Main Dramatics GC, Sophie prod, or PAF, Hostel two always leaves its mark.

Lit@H2

The lit culture in hostel has been really great and many of the best writers and quizzers of institute reside in hostel 2. For last few years, hostel 2 has been dominating the literary arts competitions.

Film@H2

Many good filmmakers and editors call hostel 2 their home. The residents of hostel 2 have been playing important role in film-making culture of institute. This year, h2 won 3rd position in sophie film GC.

Speaking Arts@H2

Debating has been a very important part of hostel culture. A regular place for institute debaters to practice, H2 has played a major role in debate culture in the campus. With some of the best speakers of institute residing in hostel 2, it has high hopes in speaking arts.

PFA@H2

With some of the best artists designers and photographers in the institute, hostel 2 has had a very elaborate culture of photography and fine arts.
