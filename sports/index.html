Sports@H2



Hostel 2 is located at the epicenter of Gymkhana Grounds, New and Old SAC and other sports complexes, is equipped well with a Footer field, a semi Basketball court, Volleyball court, Badminton court, a cricket pitch and a frisbee field. It nurtures in it sports enthusiasts from sophies to post graduates. Hostel is also facilitated by a lounge that possesses a TT table and carrom boards and also contains a Pool room to add to its versatility.

A centralised system for issuing sports equipments has also been introduced in the hostel this year that consists of the following equipments : Badminton, TT, Tennis, squash Rackets, shuttles, Footballs, Volleyballs, a basketball, chess boards, Hockey sticks, Tennis balls, last but not the least cricket bats. These equipments could be issued by a hostelite against his I-card at any hour of the day. Currently we are leading the overall Sports GC due to the active participation of the inmates, the enthusiasm of the council and the sports culture in the Hostel.

Apart from this, Inter wing Competitions are organised on regular basis. Intra competitions were organised for football, Chess, Carrom, Tug of war so far.
