Facilities



We work to make your life comfortable in “The Wild Ones”. We have various facilities in our Hostel.

    Lounge

  • The place where inmates can have relaxation after busy Academic schedule. We have TT table, Carrom Boards, TV with Tata Sky HD connection, a big size mirror for dance practice, News Papers and Magazines.

    • TV Room

  • The stairs near the canteen goes to TV room. We have a big screen TV with Tata Sky HD connection. We have Home Theater System connected to the TV. We also have comfortable sofas, chairs and cots inside the room.

    • Washing and Ironing room

  • The room behind mess area where we can give the clothes to washerman for washing, drying and ironing or wash the clothes by ourselves. Also we have washing machines and dryers at second floor of each wing.

    • Extra

  • We have vacuum cleaner which can be issued on I-card.
  • There are 4 Hammocks in our hostel, two at Cricket field and two at Basketball court.
  • We have cycle air compressor near to security guard which is in good condition.
  • We have canteen, stationary and Xerox shop inside the hostel
  • Hostel room no. 2 and 3 are available for guests which can be booked on the Hostel Guest Room Booking link.

All these facilities are for inmates and we expect that all students use them responsibly, so that all of us can make the best use of these.
