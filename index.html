Lan Complaints

 The Vendor will visit every 2nd & 4th Saturday of a month in the morning. The concerned person should be present during the vendor visit. If the student is not available in the room, it will be treated as locked and system administrator is not responsible for it. If the room is locked it will be served in next vendor visit or at the end if time permits. Please fill the the form with appropriate Subject and Message atleast 3 days prior to the scheduled visit date.

 CMS can be used for all common complaints(Hostel and Institute). The complaints lodged via the CMS are automatically emailed to the concerned authorities and the status will be emailed to you.
