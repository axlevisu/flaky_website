Letter to Alumni

Dear Hostel 2 alumnus,

An alumnus is very important to any institution. The heights achieved by the institution is due to years of gard work and the legacy left behind by the Alumni. HOSTEL 2 is a strong example of IIT Bombay's merits. It is the alumni due to which we have a very strong hostel culture making it the best.

No matter in whichever corner of the world you reside, Hostel 2 will always be your second home. You can come and be here any time you want. The hostel gates are always be open for you. You are very important for the current inmates of the hostel as it is your experience and knowledge about the outer world that will help students tackle internships, placements with confidence and above all, live life "king size".

The inmates of hostel 2 are continuing the legacy of The Wild Ones in every aspect. We are leading in Sports GC with back to back golds in chess, badminton, table tennis, Triathlon GC and 4th in crossy GC. In Culturals, we came third in Gyrations, and 4th in Sophie Dance League.

During last few years, there have been a number of developments in the hostel like every wing has got it's own dryer.

Our music room has been made sound-proof due to alumni contributions.

One place where we are lagging is in the Mess Workers Fund, we are in a debt of ₹4,35,000 due to the loan taken from other hostels a few years back.

Finally we would like to request you to be a part of HATS fund and help your hostel as "we are nothing without your help !" Here is the link for becoming a part of IITB HATS Team.

Regards,

Pariichay Limbodia,
